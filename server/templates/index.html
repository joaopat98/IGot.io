The Game

The goals and mechanics of the game are extremely simple: you, the player, have to shoot the other players in order to make points and win the game. HOWEVER, there's a catch: among the human players, there will be bots disguised as other players running around. The goal is to find out who are the human players and kill them before they kill you, but if you kill a bot you lose points.

Controls

Move the character across the map. If you can't beat them, join them.

Control the aim to the right and left, respectively. Make sure to step up your aim!

Shoot the characters you think are real players. Can you handle the bluff?
